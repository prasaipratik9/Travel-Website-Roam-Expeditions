Travel Guides

Visas & Documents

Check passport validity (6+ months), apply for visas early, and keep digital/printed copies of all documents.

Connectivity

Compare local eSIMs vs. roaming; download offline maps and language packs before you go.

Stay aware, not afraid

Safety Tips

Health & Insurance

Carry a basic first-aid kit and confirm your coverage for medical emergencies abroad.

Belongings

Use hotel safes; keep valuables in cross-body bags; avoid displaying cash or flashy items.

Travel lighter, move faster

Packing Tips

The 5–4–3–2–1 Rule

Five tops, four bottoms, three accessories, two shoes, one hat/jacket—adapt by trip length.

Essentials

Universal adapter, power bank, refillable bottle, meds, and a tiny laundry kit.

Make the most of timing

Seasonal Tips

Peak vs. Shoulder

Shoulder seasons mean milder weather, fewer crowds, and better prices.

Weather Ready

Layer up; pack a compact rain jacket; check forecasts 48 hours before departure.
